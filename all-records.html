All Records — EMS data visibility & explainability

0→1 platform surface that makes product data discoverable, trustworthy, and explainable: what exists, when it changed, and what automation acted—so anyone can self-serve root cause.

63% drop in weekly open data tickets (73 → 27)
#2 most-viewed interface
~80% of primary app’s traffic

Problem → Insight

“Silent failures” and data opacity caused avoidable escalations and churn risk. Users couldn’t easily see which entities were available, when they were last updated, or why an automation did or didn’t fire. Common support work involved ad-hoc SQL queries just to verify freshness and source-of-truth.

  • List & search across EMS entities, not just people records.
  • Show Date Added / Date Updated and provenance at a glance.
  • Join to timelines & audits for one-click “why.”
My role PM (Platform) — product strategy, requirements, design partnering, delivery, and org alignment.

Outcomes

Ticket reduction
Open data tickets dropped from a peak of 73 to 27 after launch (≈63% ↓). Many issues now resolve via self-serve.
Adoption
~80% of the primary app’s traffic; #2 most-viewed interface across customers and internal teams.

What I built

  • Catalog — all EMS entities with search (IDs & names), sortable freshness (Date Added/Updated).
  • Profile — per-record detail (Sense Data), associated entities, and subscriptions.
  • Timeline/Activity — explainability: what automation touched a record and when.
  • Link-outs — debugger paths for deeper root-cause (optional; separate tool).
  • Guardrails — design iterations (hide nulls, clearer scopes, nav moves).
From “black box” to explainable data: see it, trust it, act—without a ticket.

Delivery & influence

  • Rollout: feature-flagged; enablement for CS/IM; release notes and support articles.
  • Org friction: concern that “showing more data would create tickets.” I ran discovery sessions, piloted with high-need accounts, and proved the opposite: deflection through explainability.
  • Rituals: shipped alongside drift/anomaly checks and weekly reliability reviews to prevent regression.

Design notes and early feedback (hide nulls, date scopes, navigation) guided multiple iterations.
