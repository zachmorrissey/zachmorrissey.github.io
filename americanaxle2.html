ABOUT AAM AAM was built to deliver POWER, but we live to create opportunity — for others, and for ourselves. For over 25 years, our dedication to Quality, Operational Excellence and Technology Leadership has helped us grow into a tier one global automotive supplier with over 20,000 associates, approximately 80 facilities and 15 engineering centers in 18 countries. Our focus on sustainability means we’re committed to our planet; and our focus on our community is proof that we believe in making a positive impact everywhere we live and work.
